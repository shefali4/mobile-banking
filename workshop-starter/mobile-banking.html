Mobile Banking

Banking on the go.

Mobile Banking Apps

Download our free mobile banking app for easy access to your accounts wherever you go.  Apple or Android, smartphone or iPad, you can access our convenient mobile banking options 24/7 to check account balances, transfer funds between Civista accounts or Bank to Bank, pay bills and people, freeze your debit card, and much more.1


Mobile App Demo


Security at your fingertips

For added security, biometric authentication is available for both Android and iOS compatible devices.  You even have the option to suspend or activate your card instantly using the card management feature in the Preference settings.  This is a great option to consider when you are not using your card.


Deposit checks on the go

With Civista's free Mobile Deposit you can deposit checks directly to your personal checking or savings account right from your mobile device 24/7.  You'll save time and trips to the bank or ATM to make a deposit.
